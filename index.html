Welcome!

I am a recent graduate of Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), where I did my bachelors in Computer Science. I am currently a Research Assistant at the LUMS Center for Speech and Language Technologies (CSaLT), working under the guidance of Dr. Agha Ali Raza. Additionally, I also serve as the Technical Lead at AI for Healthcare Initiative Lab (AIHI).


My research interests and work revolve around designing for development, specifically leveraging AI & ML to tackle complex social and environmental challenges. In particular, my work spans the realm of health tech, encompassing themes of general wellbeing, mental health and physical health.


My research goals consist of creating sustainable design solutions that prioritize accessibility and inclusivity, especially for underrepresented communities.

Selected Projects

Darcheeni

ressearch project: on-going

Darcheeni is an AI-enabled physician assistant (AIPA) which listens to the doctor-patient conversation, elicits contextualized medical decision-making and develops a personalized Assessment and Plan (A&P) for each patient.

Navigating Anger: A Religio-Spiritual Exploration

research project: on-going

This project aims to explore anger management in Pakistan, particularly from the unique and under-researched lens of religious and spiritual beliefs.

Polarity

course project: completed

A user-centered design approach to recognize the primary needs of people on the Bipolar spectrum to devise an efficient, all-in-one record-keeping strategy. Five major themes were identified: usability, customizability, adherence, clinical intervention, and privacy.
